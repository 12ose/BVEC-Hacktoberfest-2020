DEPARTMENT OF COMPUTER SCIENCE AND ENGINEERING

About the Department:

The Department of Computer Science and Engineering was established in April, 2017. The department offers Bachelor of Engineering (BE) degree under Gauhati University with an intake capacity of 60 students per year which is approved by AICTE. The department has a national level course curriculum whichever is well structured and also laboratory resources that are useful for students. The department also has a well equipped computer centre which provides theaceous computing facilities to all the students of the college.

Vision:

To provide an adequate platform for learning and research in computer science and engineering for developing competent computer science and and Telecommunication engineering education where the best of teaching-learning, state-of-art research and consultancy synergize. engineering graduates who will contribute productively to local, national as well as global economies.

Mission:

  1. To provide good quality education along with state-of- the-art laboratory facilities.
  2. To enable the students as well as the faculty members to achieve the department goals.
  3. To continuously upgrade the curriculum and laboratory facilities to train the students in latest technology for better employability.
  4. To involve the students in research and development areas in individual as well as team projects. the department as well as in the institution's so that the students ingrain professional values and ethical behaviour.

DEPARTMENT OF ELECTRONICS AND TELECOMMUNICATION ENGINEERING

About the Department:

The Department offers B.E degree in Electronics in Telecommunication Engineering.The department emphasizes on technology oriented courses, quality education, training andresearch orientedcourse at the undergraduate level in various areas of Electronics andTelecommunication Engineering. The department's Curriculum programme includes a deeperstudy of electronics engineering sciences to which the students are introduced at the core level,experimental solution of physical problems, techniques & method of design of system relevant tothe contemporary industrial world. The Department has a class of qualified, young, dynamic anddedicated faculty members. The department has excellent infrastructure and serene atmospherethat grooms the prospective teachers to evolve their best and shape their future. At present, thedepartment has student strength of 60 (sixty).

Vision:

To be regionally, nationally and internationally recognized centre of excellence in allfields of Electronics and Telecommunication engineering education where the best of teaching-learning, state-of-art research and consultancy synergize.

Mission:

  1. To create academic excellence by imparting quality teaching, carrying out research and technology development in frontier areas of Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering.
  2. To inculcate the spirit of innovation and creativity by active participation in development activities.
  3. To promote enterprising abilities for the benefit of society by imbibing professional skills.
